About Us

We Keep Your Pets Happy All Time

TemPaw Care is a dedicated organization that specializes in providing temporary care for animals. Our mission is to ensure the well-being and safety of pets when their owners are unable to look after them temporarily. With a team of passionate animal lovers and a network of trusted caregivers, we offer a range of services to ensure that animals receive the attention, love, and care they need during their owners' absence.
